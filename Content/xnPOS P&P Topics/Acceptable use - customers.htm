Purpose

This Policy describes permissible and impermissible use of the xnPOS business solution and to ensure that users act in compliance with applicable laws, rules and regulations.

Scope

This Acceptable use policy ("AUP") applies to Customer use of and access to any XPS hosted application services or products (“Services”) made available through XPS managed data centres. The Customer agrees to comply with this AUP by using Services, which may be amended, modified or updated from time to time.

Controls

Customer obligations

Customers will only make Services available to those end users using the Service (“Users”) and will ensure that each User only accesses the Service using his or her own unique account credentials. Customers will ensure that the Service is only accessed through interfaces or other automated means approved by XPS.

Customers will ensure that individual User accounts are not used by another person or entity.

Customers will not bypass, deactivate or otherwise circumvent any access controls or security measures for the Service.

Customers will not engage in any activity that disrupts, diminishes the quality of, interferes with the performance of, or impairs Service functionality.

Customers will not attempt to gain unauthorised access to or otherwise interfere with or disrupt XPS networks, systems, data or operations.

Customers will ensure nothing is transmitted or downloaded by or on behalf of the Customer in breach or violation of:

  1. any National or International law, regulation or treaty;
  2. the terms of this AUP; or,
  3. the intellectual property or rights of any person.

Customers will not use the Service for transmission or storage of:

  1. computer viruses, trojan horses or other malicious code;
  2. unlawful or tortuous material;
  3. material in violation of third-party privacy rights or other contractual, fiduciary or legal duty or obligation; or
  4. other material which is defamatory or abusive or of an obscene or threatening character, or which may cause inconvenience or needless anxiety in a manner which constitutes a violation or infringement of the rights of any person, firm or company (including but not limited to intellectual property rights).

Customers acknowledge that XPS is unable to exercise control over the content of any information passing through the Service and that XPS does not monitor or exercise any editorial control over the information passing through the Service. The Customer further acknowledges that XPS hereby excludes all liability in respect of any transmission or reception of information by the Customer or an End User, of whatever kind, or the accuracy of the contents thereof, or the scrambling of any information or data.

In the event that a Customer is in breach of any provision of this AUP, XPS reserves the right to suspend the Service forthwith without notice.

Control exceptions

All exemption requests must be reviewed, assessed and approved by the relevant business stakeholders

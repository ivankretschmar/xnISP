Mission Statement

Xn protel Systems is committed to preserving the confidentiality, integrity and availability of all key information assets in order to maintain its competitive edge, legal and contractual compliance and reputation. The XPS Information Security Program shall be the enabling mechanism for reducing information-related risk to an acceptable level.
